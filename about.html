About Us

Who we are and why we do this.

Purpose

SecWonks exists to provide a trusted, informal community for current and former Higher Education security professionals.

Our purpose is to maintain relationships beyond institutional boundaries, facilitate thoughtful exchange of security insight, and preserve the collaborative spirit that defines security work in Higher Education.

Mission

We connect experienced security practitioners to:

  • Share high-level insights, trends, and lessons learned
  • Provide peer perspective on emerging risks and strategic challenges
  • Maintain professional relationships beyond specific institutions
  • Support one another’s continued growth and career evolution

Guiding Principles

Trust First

Conversations are grounded in professional discretion. Members respect confidentiality and do not share sensitive information outside the group without permission.

Experience Matters

Discussions reflect the strategic and operational realities of Higher Education security. We value practical insight over posturing.

No Vendor Pitching

SecWonks is a peer community, not a sales channel. Members do not use the forum for solicitation or marketing.

Candid, Respectful Dialogue

We encourage honest discussion while maintaining professionalism and mutual respect.

Institutional Neutrality

Views expressed are personal and do not represent employers, past or present.

If you have any questions feel free to contact us at secwonks@protonmail.com

Security Wonks is published independently. Views expressed are those of individual authors.
