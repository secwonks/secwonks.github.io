Slack

In 2017, a group of Higher Education security professionals worked together across campuses, defending networks and arguing about network monitoring.


After years of incidents, audits, and budget meetings, they scattered across the industry — but refused to lose touch.


Today, if you’re looking for them… if you can find them… you can probably find them on Slack.

SecWonks hosts an informal Slack workspace where we socialize.


The Slack Workspace is invite only. Please reach out to an existing member for an invite or send us an email at secwonks@protonmail.com.

SecWonks Slack – Rules & Etiquette

  1. Respect Confidentiality
    Do not share sensitive institutional information or anything discussed here outside the workspace without permission.
  2. Keep It Professional
    Be respectful. Debate ideas, not people.
  3. No Vendor Pitching
    This is a peer community, not a sales channel. No unsolicited promotion or marketing.
  4. Share Responsibly
    When discussing incidents or security issues, keep details high-level and sanitized.
  5. Stay On Topic
    Use channels appropriately and keep discussions relevant to the group’s purpose.
  6. Be Helpful
    Share knowledge, ask questions, and support fellow members.
  7. Assume Good Intent
    We’re all here because of shared experience in Higher Ed security.
  8. Have a Little Fun
    This is a professional community, but it’s also a social one.
